How to Make the Perfect Cup of Coffee

Making the perfect cup of coffee is a matter of personal preference, but here are some basic steps to get you started. 1. Start with fresh, high-quality coffee beans. A good rule of thumb is to use two tablespoons of ground coffee for every six ounces of water. 2. Bring fresh, cold water to a boil in a pot or a kettle. 3. Once the water is boiling, pour it into a French press or filter and let it sit for a few minutes to reach its optimal temperature of 195-205°F. 4. Add the ground coffee into your French press or filter and give it a light stir. 5. Let the coffee steep for 3-4 minutes. 6. Once the steep time is up, press down the French press plunger or filter the coffee from the pot. 7. Pour the coffee into a mug and add milk, sugar, or any other desired ingredients. 8. Enjoy your freshly brewed cup of coffee!
